Project
NS/urveillance

My goal in this project is to bring awareness to the issue of government surveillance by confronting students at The New School with images of themselves being surveilled. It is meant to be provoking and make the user question what it means to lose their right to privacy.

Initial design question I framed my project around.

Goal of what I wanted to provoke my users to think about and question.

Process of carrying out project concept. This is one area I hid the mini camera to surveil the New School students.

Snapshots of students captured by the hidden camera.

Images captured from hidden camera were used to develop these posters.

Posters were then hidden in the area that the pictures were originally taken. The posters included the name of an instagram account explaining the project and showing all images captured.

Screenshot of the ProjectSurveillance instagram account.

Quotes included on the ProjectNSurveillance account to explain inspiration behind project.

Iteration 2 of the posters. Wrote the word "follow" on them so people would know to go to instagram.

Iteration 3 of the posters. Included instagram icon to make it more clear to users to go to the instagram account.

Unexpected results of project include people tearing down posters of themselves or of their friends. Case in point: in this image someone took away one of the posters instead of both of them, suggesting they cared about the content of the poster rather than taking them down because no posters are supposed to be in that area.

Unexpected results of project include people tearing down posters of themselves or of their friends. A few hours after posting posters almost half of them were gone. A week later a handful of posters remained on the walls.
